About & Methodology

Country Macro Intelligence visualizes GDP and bilateral trade for the world's largest economies. Bubbles are countries (size and color encode economic weight); lines are bilateral trade flows. Select a sector to rank the top 10 producers, or filter by trade bloc.

Data Sources
